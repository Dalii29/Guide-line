Email "Send As" Configuration Guide

Step-by-step instructions to set up email alias sending for different clients

  • Thunderbird
  • Outlook
  • Google Mail

Mozilla Thunderbird

  1. How to download Thunderbird

    2. 1. Open "any browser" on your device.
    2. Search for ''Thunderbird''
    3. Find the ''Thunderbird'' app, click “Download”
    4. Wait for the app to finish downloading.

  2. How to set up email Mozilla Thunderbird
  3. Open Thunderbird and go to Account Settings from the menu or by right-clicking your account.
  4. In the Account Settings window, click on "Manage Identities" button.
  5. In the Identities window:
    • Click "Add" to create a new identity
    • Set the email address to demo@your-name.com
    • Configure other settings as needed (name, organization, etc.)
  6. Click "OK" to save changes, then restart Thunderbird for changes to take effect.
Note: Thunderbird may take a few minutes to fully apply the changes after restart.

Microsoft Outlook

  1. How to download outlook.

    2. 1. *For Android, open 'Play Store' on your device.
    *For iOS open "App Store" on your device.
    2. Search for ''outlook'' and click on the search icon.
    3. Find the ''outlook'' app, click “Install” for Android, and click “Get” for iOS.
    4. Wait for the app to finish downloading.


  2. How to set up email Microsoft Outlook

    3. Enter your email address and password then click "Next"

    Verify your email address

  3. Go to File > Account Settings > Account Settings.
  4. Select your primary email account from the list and click "Change".
  5. In the Change Account window:
    • Click "More Settings"
    • Navigate to the "Send As" tab
    • Enable the option and enter demo@your-name.com
  6. Click "OK", then "Next" and "Finish". Restart Outlook to apply changes.
Important: Outlook may require administrator privileges to make these changes.

Google Mail (Gmail)

  1. How to download outlook.

    2. 1. *For Android, open 'Play Store' on your device.
    *For iOS open "App Store" on your device.
    2. Search for ''Gmail'' and click on the search icon.
    3. Find the ''Gmail'' app, click “Install” for Android, and click “Get” for iOS.
    4. Wait for the app to finish downloading.


  2. How to set up email Microsoft Outlook

    3. Enter your email address and password then click "Next"

    To sign out clicksign out off all accounts

  3. Click the gear icon and select "See all settings", then go to the "Accounts and Import" tab.
  4. In the "Send mail as" section, click "Add another email address".
  5. In the popup window:
    • Enter your name and the email address demo@your-name.com
    • Uncheck "Treat as an alias" if you want distinct sender addresses
    • Click "Next Step"
  6. Complete the verification process:
    • Gmail will send a verification code to the new address
    • Enter the code when prompted
    • Click "Verify"
  7. Once verified, you can select the sender address when composing new emails.
Tip: The verification process typically takes 1-2 minutes. If you don't receive the code, check your spam folder.
